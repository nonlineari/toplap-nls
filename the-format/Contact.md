## Strategic Recommendations

### Maximize Revenue
1. **Early Bird NFT Sales** - Create urgency and initial capital
2. **Sponsorship Priority** - Secure web3 brand partnerships early
3. **VIP Tiering** - Offer multiple price points for accessibility
4. **Merchandise Pre-Orders** - Reduce inventory risk

### Minimize Costs
1. **Leverage Open Source** - Use free/low-cost streaming platforms initially
2. **Community Marketing** - Organic social media through executive network
3. **Shared Resources** - Partner with existing web3 platforms for infrastructure
4. **Phased Rollout** - Start small, scale based on revenue

### Long-Term Sustainability
1. **Recurring Revenue** - Membership models, subscription content
2. **Secondary Market Royalties** - Build in 5-10% on NFT resales
3. **Content Archive** - Monetize past event recordings
4. **Brand Partnerships** - Establish ongoing sponsor relationships
